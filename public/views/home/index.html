About Language Course

Today the school is a leader in the market of educational services and is the largest language school.

comfortable learning environment

Training individually and in groups. The group consist of not more than 8 people, that allows to make learning as effective as possible. Teaching in nine specialized classes.

innovative online system

Regardless of whether you're on a lesson or not by going to our website, you can learn the theme of the last sessions, view and download all the necessary materials, as well as get homework.

Qualitative educational process

You are taught by professional teachers, constantly improving their skills. Based on the communicative approach, we combine the most effective trends in world practice.

Curricula and methods are suitable for children (from 10 years) and adults. From general and express any level courses to preparing for international exams.
